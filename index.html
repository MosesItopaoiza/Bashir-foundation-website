About Us

Founded in 2018, our mission is to support women affected by obstetric fistula through advocacy, education, and surgical care.

What is Obstetric Fistula?

A childbirth injury caused by prolonged labor, leading to a hole between the vagina and bladder or rectum. Common causes include lack of access to healthcare and harmful practices.

Global Impact

  • 🌍 3 million women affected worldwide
  • 🇳🇬 400,000 in Nigeria
  • 📈 50,000–100,000 new cases annually

Our Impact

We empower women through education, healthcare, and community support.
